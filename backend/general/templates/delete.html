Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account in the Trendy Phrases app, please follow the instructions below. This process will remove your profile and other personal data from our servers.

How to Delete Your Account

1. Open the Trendy Phrases app and navigate to your user profile.

2. Within your profile, locate and press the "Delete Account" button.

3. You will receive a confirmation dialog. Confirm your decision to permanently delete your account.

Data Deletion Details

Upon deleting your account, all personal information associated with your account will be permanently removed from our servers. This includes:

  • Your profile information (e.g., name, username, and email address)
  • Any other personal data collected through your use of the app

We do not retain any personal data beyond what is necessary for legal or regulatory purposes. Any data kept for these reasons will be held for a period no longer than required by law.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, or if you need assistance, please contact our support team at support@trendyphrases.com.
